# EasyBee OGSM

## O — Objective
Give Vietnamese nail salon workers in America the English confidence to survive, function, and thrive at work and in daily life — through structured, context-specific practice they can't find anywhere else.

## G — Goals

### 6-month goals
- Module 1 (Nail Salon English) complete: 63 lessons, 7 classes, A1→B2
- 100 beta users (real nail salon workers) using the app weekly
- App on App Store (Capacitor wrap) with working speech recognition
- 1 additional module started (Healthcare or Emergency)

### 12-month goals
- 4 modules complete (~250 lessons)
- 1,000 monthly active users
- Paid tier launched (per-module unlock or subscription)
- CEFR progression tracking (Phase 3 Supabase)

## S — Strategies

1. **Content-first** — Ship complete Module 1 before building fancy features. Content is the product. UX is the delivery vehicle.

2. **Nail salon workers, not "ESL learners"** — Every lesson must answer "when would I use this at the salon?" Generic ESL = competition. Nail-salon-specific = monopoly.

3. **Speech over text** — Target users learn by hearing and speaking, not reading. TTS + speech recognition is the core loop, not flashcards.

4. **Mobile-only, touch-first** — No desktop. No complex UI. One hand, 10 minutes, between customers. That's the usage pattern.

5. **Free Module 1, paid expansion** — Hook with the core product (nail salon), monetize with life skills (healthcare, shopping, workplace rights).

6. **Community distribution** — Vietnamese nail salon Facebook groups, TikTok Vietnamese nail community, word of mouth inside salons. Zero marketing budget needed for first 1,000 users.
